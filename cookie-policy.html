1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device (computer, tablet, or mobile) when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently and provide information to website owners.

How Cookies Work

When you visit our website, cookies are downloaded to your device. These files contain information that is read by our website on subsequent visits, allowing us to:

  • Remember your preferences and settings
  • Improve website functionality and performance
  • Analyze how you use our website
  • Provide personalized content and advertisements

Types of Cookie Information

Cookies may contain information such as:

  • Unique identifier codes
  • Website usage patterns
  • User preferences and settings
  • Login status and session information
  • Shopping cart contents (if applicable)

2. Why We Use Cookies

We use cookies for several important purposes:

3. Types of Cookies We Use

We categorize cookies based on their function and duration:

By Function

Essential Cookies

Required for basic website functionality

Analytics Cookies

Help us understand website usage

Marketing Cookies

Used for advertising and personalization

Preference Cookies

Remember your settings and choices

By Duration

  • Session Cookies: Temporary cookies that are deleted when you close your browser
  • Persistent Cookies: Remain on your device for a set period or until manually deleted

By Origin

  • First-Party Cookies: Set directly by our website
  • Third-Party Cookies: Set by external services we use (like Google Analytics)

8. Managing Your Cookie Preferences

Our Cookie Preference Center

You can manage your cookie preferences at any time using our Cookie Preference Center, accessible through:

  • The cookie banner that appears on your first visit
  • The "Cookie Settings" link in our website footer
  • Your account settings (if you have an account)

Browser Cookie Settings

You can also control cookies through your browser settings:

Google Chrome

  1. Click the three-dot menu → Settings
  2. Go to Privacy and Security → Cookies and other site data
  3. Choose your preferred cookie settings

Mozilla Firefox

  1. Click the menu button → Options
  2. Select Privacy & Security
  3. Adjust settings under Cookies and Site Data

Safari

  1. Click Safari → Preferences
  2. Go to the Privacy tab
  3. Adjust cookie and website data settings

Microsoft Edge

  1. Click the three-dot menu → Settings
  2. Go to Cookies and site permissions
  3. Manage cookies and site data

Mobile Device Settings

For mobile devices:

  • iOS: Settings → Safari → Block All Cookies
  • Android: Chrome app → Settings → Site settings → Cookies

Impact of Disabling Cookies

Disabling certain cookies may affect your experience:

  • Essential cookies: Website may not function properly
  • Analytics cookies: We cannot improve our services based on usage data
  • Marketing cookies: You may see less relevant advertisements
  • Preference cookies: Settings and preferences will not be saved
