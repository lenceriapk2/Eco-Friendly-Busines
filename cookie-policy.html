Cookie Policy

Last updated: December 2025

1. What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your computer or mobile device when you visit our website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently and provide a better user experience.

2. How We Use Cookies

EcoSustainable.co.uk uses cookies for the following purposes:

  • Essential Cookies: These are necessary for the website to function properly
  • Analytics Cookies: These help us understand how visitors interact with our website
  • Functional Cookies: These enable enhanced functionality and personalization
  • Performance Cookies: These help us improve website performance

3. Types of Cookies We Use

3.1 Session Cookies

These are temporary cookies that are deleted when you close your browser. They help us recognize you as you navigate between pages.

3.2 Persistent Cookies

These cookies remain on your device for a set period or until you delete them. They help us remember your preferences when you return to our site.

4. Third-Party Cookies

We may use third-party services that set cookies on our website, including:

  • Google Analytics for website analytics
  • Social media platforms for sharing content
  • Advertising networks for relevant advertisements

5. Managing Cookies

You can control and manage cookies in various ways:

  • Through your browser settings to block or delete cookies
  • By using our cookie preference center when available
  • By opting out of third-party analytics services

6. Cookie Consent

By continuing to use our website, you consent to our use of cookies as described in this policy. You can withdraw your consent at any time by adjusting your browser settings.

7. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

8. Contact Us
