About EcoSustainable.co.uk

Connecting environmentally conscious consumers with verified sustainable businesses across the UK

Our Mission

At EcoSustainable.co.uk, we believe that every purchasing decision is an opportunity to support businesses that prioritize environmental responsibility. Our mission is to make it easier for UK consumers to discover, connect with, and support sustainable businesses in their local communities and beyond.

What We Do

  • Verify and curate eco-friendly businesses across the UK
  • Provide detailed sustainability information for each business
  • Connect consumers with local sustainable alternatives
  • Support small and medium businesses in their sustainability journey

Our Story

Founded in 2024, EcoSustainable.co.uk emerged from a simple observation: while more UK businesses were adopting sustainable practices, consumers often struggled to find them. Our founders, passionate about environmental conservation and supporting local businesses, saw an opportunity to bridge this gap.

What started as a small directory of eco-friendly businesses in London has grown into the UK's most comprehensive platform for sustainable business discovery. Today, we feature hundreds of verified businesses across 79 UK cities, spanning multiple categories from renewable energy providers to zero-waste retailers.

Our Values

Transparency

We provide clear, honest information about each business's sustainability practices, certifications, and environmental impact.

Community

We believe in building strong connections between sustainable businesses and environmentally conscious consumers.

Growth

Impact

Every connection we facilitate contributes to a more sustainable future for the UK and beyond.
