Our Story

Founded in 2024, EcoSustainable.co.uk emerged from a simple observation: while more UK businesses were adopting sustainable practices, consumers often struggled to find them.

Our founders, passionate about environmental conservation and supporting local businesses, saw an opportunity to bridge this gap. What started as a small directory of eco-friendly businesses in London has grown into the UK's most comprehensive platform for sustainable business discovery.

Today, we feature hundreds of verified businesses across 79 UK cities, spanning multiple categories from renewable energy providers to zero-waste retailers.
